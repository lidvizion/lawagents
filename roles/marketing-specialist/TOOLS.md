## Ethics Compliance

- **Bar rules:** Check state bar advertising rules before new channels.
- **Testimonials:** Document client consent; avoid misleading claims.
- **Solicitation:** No improper solicitation; no false/misleading statements.
- **Review:** Partner or counsel reviews before launch for new channels.
