# Business Development Lead

**Category:** Executive and Management Staff  
**Approval Level:** Medium—campaigns and major initiatives need partner approval

---

## Role Summary

The Business Development Lead attracts clients and manages the firm's business development strategy. They oversee marketing, client intake process design, content creation, and pipeline development. In smaller firms, this may be combined with Marketing Specialist; in larger firms, the BD Lead sets strategy while Marketing executes. Focus on origination and revenue growth.

---

## Detailed Responsibilities

### 1. Business Development Strategy

- **Pipeline development:** Identify and pursue new business opportunities
- **Target markets:** Define target clients, industries, and practice areas
- **Competitive positioning:** Differentiate firm; value proposition
- **Growth initiatives:** New practice areas, geographic expansion, lateral hires
- **Partner support:** Support partners on pitches, proposals, and relationship development

### 2. Client Intake and Conversion

- **Intake process design:** Optimize intake for conversion and efficiency
- **CRM strategy:** Clio Grow or similar—pipeline, lead scoring, follow-up
- **Conversion metrics:** Track conversion rates; identify bottlenecks
- **Referral program:** Develop and manage referral relationships
- **RFP responses:** Coordinate RFP responses; ensure quality and timeliness

### 3. Marketing and Content

- **Online marketing:** Website, SEO, paid advertising
- **Content creation:** Thought leadership, alerts, seminars, webinars
- **Social media:** LinkedIn, etc.—firm presence and partner support
- **Events:** Seminars, networking, sponsorships
- **Analytics:** Track campaign performance; ROI; report to partners

### 4. Brand and Communications

- **Brand management:** Ensure consistent brand across all touchpoints
- **Communications:** Internal and external; support firm messaging
- **Ethics compliance:** Ensure all BD and marketing complies with bar rules
- **Crisis communications:** Support as needed
