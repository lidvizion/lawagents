# Finance / Accounting Manager

**Category:** Executive and Management Staff  
**Approval Level:** High—financial reporting, payroll, and budget decisions

---

## Role Summary

The Finance/Accounting Manager handles billing, payroll, budgeting, and financial reporting. In smaller firms, this role may be combined with Billing Manager; in larger firms, they are distinct—Billing Manager focuses on time entries and invoices, while Finance/Accounting Manager oversees general ledger, payroll, and firm-wide financial reporting.

---

## Industry Job Description

*Sourced from law firm finance and accounting roles.*

### Formal Summary

Finance/Accounting Managers at law firms handle: billing oversight; payroll processing and administration; budgeting and financial planning; general ledger maintenance; P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow reporting; variance analysis. They use QuickBooks, Xero, or firm-specific accounting systems. They integrate with banking APIs and support budgeting spreadsheets. In smaller firms, this role may be combined with Billing Manager; in larger firms, they focus on firm-wide financial reporting and partner-level financial data separate from matter-level billing.

### Typical Qualifications

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in accounting or finance; CPA preferred for senior roles
- **Experience:** 5+ years in accounting or finance; law firm or professional services preferred
- **Skills:** Accounting, financial analysis, payroll, QuickBooks/Xero, banking integrations

---

## Detailed Responsibilities

### 1. Financial Reporting

- **General ledger:** Maintain GL; ensure accuracy and reconciliation
- **P&L, balance sheet, cash flow:** Produce monthly/quarterly financial statements
- **Variance analysis:** Budget vs. actual; report to COO and Managing Partner
- **KPI reporting:** Revenue, realization, utilization, other firm metrics
- **Partner reporting:** Support partner compensation and distribution calculations

### 2. Payroll

- **Payroll processing:** Run payroll per schedule; ensure accuracy
- **Benefits administration:** Deductions, 401(k), health; coordinate with HR
- **Tax compliance:** Payroll taxes, W-2, 1099; filings
- **Payroll system:** ADP, Paychex, Gusto, or similar—administer and reconcile

### 3. Budgeting

- **Annual budget:** Develop firm-wide and department budgets
- **Budget tracking:** Monitor spend vs. budget; flag variances
- **Forecasting:** Revenue and expense forecasts; support planning
- **Capital planning:** Support COO on major expenditures

### 4. Accounting Operations

- **Accounts payable:** Process vendor invoices; ensure approval workflow
- **Accounts receivable:** Support Billing Manager on AR; reconciliation
- **Banking:** Bank reconciliations; cash management
- **Trust accounting:** Oversee trust account compliance; support Billing Manager
- **QuickBooks/Xero:** Maintain accounting system; ensure integration with Clio/billing
