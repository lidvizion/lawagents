# Non-Equity / Junior Partners

**Category:** Attorneys and Legal Practitioners  
**Approval Level:** Medium–High—substantive work product, client communications

---

## Role Summary

Non-equity partners are highly experienced attorneys who receive a salary rather than a share of total profits. They handle major casework, lead teams, and often serve as the primary day-to-day contact for significant matters. They are on a path to equity consideration.

---

## Detailed Responsibilities

### 1. Matter Leadership and Execution

- **Primary matter responsibility:** Serve as lead attorney on assigned matters; own case strategy and client communication under supervising partner.
- **Substantive work:** Conduct and supervise legal research, drafting, discovery, and court appearances. Maintain high quality and timeliness.
- **Client communication:** Serve as primary or secondary client contact; provide status updates, answer questions, and manage expectations.
- **Delegation:** Assign work to associates and paralegals; provide clear instructions and deadlines. Review work before partner or client delivery.

### 2. Team Management and Mentoring

- **Associate development:** Provide regular feedback, assign developmentally appropriate work, and support advancement.
- **Workload balancing:** Ensure team capacity is used effectively; flag over/underutilization to supervising partner.
- **Paralegal coordination:** Direct paralegal work; ensure substantive tasks are appropriately supervised.
- **Quality assurance:** Review drafts, research memos, and discovery responses for accuracy and completeness.

### 3. Billing and Matter Economics

- **Time entry review:** Review and approve time entries for assigned matters; ensure accuracy and appropriate narrative.
- **Invoice review:** Review draft invoices before partner approval; identify write-down opportunities and billing issues.
- **Matter budgets:** Track matter budgets; escalate when matters approach or exceed budget.
- **Realization:** Work with Billing Manager to improve realization on assigned matters.

### 4. Business Development Support

- **Pitch support:** Assist equity partners with pitches, proposals, and presentations.
- **Client retention:** Strengthen relationships with existing clients; identify cross-sell opportunities.
- **Marketing:** Contribute to firm content, seminars, and thought leadership as assigned.
- **Pipeline:** Build and maintain pipeline for future equity consideration.

### 5. Firm and Practice Participation

- **Practice meetings:** Participate in practice group meetings; contribute to process improvement and knowledge sharing.
- **Compliance:** Ensure matters comply with ethics, conflicts, and firm policies. Escalate issues promptly.
- **Matter handoffs:** Document matter status and strategy for continuity when transitioning matters.

| Skill | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| Substantive expertise | Deep knowledge in practice area; ability to handle complex matters |
| Project management | Matter timelines, deadlines, team coordination |
| Associate mentoring | Feedback, delegation, development planning |
| Client service | Responsiveness, expectation management, escalation handling |
| Case strategy | Tactical planning, risk assessment, settlement evaluation |

## Agent-Ready Tasks

| Task | Description | Guardrail |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Case research summaries | Synthesize research for partner or client | Partner reviews before reliance |
| Draft motions and briefs review | Review associate drafts; provide feedback | Partner approves before filing |
| Team task assignment | Suggest task assignments based on matter needs | Partner approves assignments |
| Billing review | Review time entries and draft invoices | Partner approves before send |
| Matter timelines | Create and maintain matter milestone schedules | Partner validates key dates |

## Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Routines

| Frequency | Activities |
|-----------|------------|
| **Daily** | Matter work; client communications; associate/paralegal direction; time entry review |
| **Weekly** | Team meetings; matter status updates; billing review; supervising partner check-in |
| **Monthly** | Matter profitability review; associate feedback; practice group participation |
| **Quarterly** | Business development activities; CLE; practice area initiatives |
| **Annually** | Performance review; compensation discussion; equity track feedback |
