# Of Counsel

**Category:** Attorneys and Legal Practitioners  
**Approval Level:** Medium—advisory output, engagement terms

---

## Role Summary

Of Counsel attorneys maintain a close, ongoing relationship with the firm but are neither partners nor associates. They typically provide niche expertise, part-time engagement, or transitional roles (e.g., senior attorneys winding down practice). They perform advisory work and matter-specific support.

---

## Detailed Responsibilities

### 1. Advisory and Substantive Work

- **Advisory memos:** Provide written or oral advice on specialized areas; document conclusions and caveats.
- **Matter-specific research:** Conduct research on discrete issues; deliver findings to supervising attorney.
- **Strategy input:** Advise on case strategy, settlement, or transaction structure. Do not make final decisions without partner approval.
- **Second opinions:** Provide independent review of complex issues when requested by partners.

### 2. Client and Matter Engagement

- **Defined scope:** Work within clearly defined scope for each matter; do not expand scope without partner approval.
- **Supervision:** All work product is reviewed by a supervising partner before client delivery.
- **Conflict screening:** Run conflict checks before accepting new matters; document results.
- **Matter handoffs:** Document work completed and recommendations when transitioning matters.

### 3. Relationship and Compliance

- **Firm policies:** Comply with firm ethics, conflicts, confidentiality, and billing policies.
- **Engagement terms:** Maintain current engagement letter or agreement defining scope, compensation, and expectations.
- **Time entry:** Enter time accurately and contemporaneously; use matter-specific narratives.
- **Professional responsibility:** Maintain malpractice insurance and bar membership as required by firm.

### 4. Knowledge Sharing (Optional)

- **Training:** Present on specialized topics to associates and partners as requested.
- **Thought leadership:** Contribute to firm content, seminars, or publications as agreed.
- **Mentoring:** Provide informal guidance to associates in area of expertise when appropriate.

## Key Skills

| Skill | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| Specialized practice area | Deep expertise in niche area (tax, IP, regulatory, etc.) |
| Advisory work | Clear written and oral advice; appropriate caveats |
| Relationship maintenance | Professional, responsive, collaborative |
| Scope management | Work within defined boundaries; escalate scope creep |

## Agent-Ready Tasks

| Task | Description | Guardrail |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Advisory memos | Draft memos with analysis and recommendations | Partner reviews before client delivery |
| Matter-specific research | Research discrete legal issues | Partner relies at own discretion |
| Conflict screening | Pre-engagement conflict checks | Attorney confirms before engagement |
| Matter handoff documentation | Summarize work, findings, and next steps | Partner reviews for continuity |

---

## Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Routines

| Frequency | Activities |
|-----------|------------|
| **As needed** | Matter work within scope; advisory input; research deliverables |
| **Weekly** | Check-in with supervising partner on active matters |
| **Monthly** | Time entry reconciliation; engagement status review |
| **Annually** | Engagement renewal; compensation discussion; scope review |
