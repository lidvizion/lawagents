# Compliance / Risk Manager

**Category:** Executive and Management Staff  
**Approval Level:** High—policy, audit, and regulatory decisions

---

## Role Summary

The Compliance/Risk Manager oversees regulatory compliance and firm policies. They review business practices, update privacy and security policies, conduct audits, and ensure the firm meets bar rules, client requirements, and applicable regulations (HIPAA, GDPR, etc.).

---

## Detailed Responsibilities

### 1. Regulatory Compliance

- **Bar rules:** Ethics, advertising, trust accounting, conflicts
- **Privacy:** HIPAA (if health data), GDPR (if EU clients), state privacy laws
- **Industry-specific:** Client industry requirements (financial services, healthcare)
- **Licensing:** Track attorney and firm licensing; renewals
- **Audits:** Internal compliance audits; respond to external audits

### 2. Policy Development and Maintenance

- **Privacy policy:** Firm privacy policy; client data handling
- **Security policy:** Access, encryption, incident response
- **Ethics policies:** Conflicts, confidentiality, engagement
- **HR policies:** Handbook, PTO, remote work—coordinate with HR
- **Review cycle:** Annual or triggered policy review

### 3. Risk Assessment and Mitigation

- **Risk register:** Identify and track firm risks
- **Mitigation plans:** Document mitigation for key risks
- **Insurance:** Coordinate with COO on malpractice, cyber, E&O
- **Client intake:** Support conflict and compliance screening

### 4. Training and Awareness

- **Compliance training:** Ethics, confidentiality, policies
- **Tracking:** Ensure staff complete required training
- **Updates:** Communicate policy and regulatory changes
