# Legal AI Marketplace

Landscape of legal AI platforms by adoption model and autopilot maturity.

---

## Marketplace Positioning

| Position | Tools | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **Emerging leader → autopilot** | Harvey | Market leader for large law firms; moving quickly to autopilot workflows |
| **Autopilot-native newcomers** | Crosby, Lawhive | Built from the ground up for agentic/autopilot workflows |

---

## Harvey

**Emerging leader.** Top legal AI for Am Law 100 and enterprise legal departments. Moving quickly to autopilot—workflows, Vault, and Knowledge increasingly run with minimal human intervention.

- [Harvey](catalog/harvey.md)
- https://www.harvey.ai/

---

## Crosby

**Autopilot-native newcomer.** AI-powered hybrid law firm combining agents with human lawyers for contract review. Agents process documents, flag risks, draft redlines; lawyers provide expert judgment. Fixed pricing, sub-hour turnaround.

- [Crosby](catalog/crosby.md)
- https://crosby.ai/

---

## Lawhive

**Autopilot-native newcomer.** UK-based AI-powered legal services provider; licensed law firm in England and Wales. Lawrence AI paralegal automates repetitive tasks. Consumer-facing; fixed-price quotes; expanding to US.

- [Lawhive](catalog/lawhive.md)
- https://lawhive.co.uk/
