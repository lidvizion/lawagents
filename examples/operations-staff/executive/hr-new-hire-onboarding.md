# HR / Recruiter: New Hire Onboarding

**Role:** [HR / Recruiter](../../../roles/hr-recruiter/README.md)  
**Frequency:** As needed (Day 1–30 for each new hire)  
**Approval:** COO or Office Manager oversees; IT grants access

---

## Scenario

Onboard a new non-attorney hire (paralegal, legal assistant, intake specialist, etc.) from offer acceptance through first 30 days.

---

## Prerequisites

- Offer accepted; start date confirmed
- Background check and any pre-employment requirements complete
- Workspace and equipment assigned

---

## Step-by-Step

### Pre-Day 1 (1–2 weeks before start)

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1.1 | Send welcome email with Day 1 details (time, place, who to ask for) | HR |
| 1.2 | Request IT to create accounts: email, PMS (Clio), phone, network | HR → IT |
| 1.3 | Order equipment (laptop, monitor, phone) if not already on hand | Office Manager |
| 1.4 | Prepare workspace (desk, chair, supplies) | Office Manager |
| 1.5 | Prepare onboarding packet (handbook, policies, benefits, W-4, I-9) | HR |
| 1.6 | Assign buddy or mentor | HR / Supervisor |

### Day 1

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 2.1 | Greet new hire; tour office; introduce to team | HR or Office Manager |
| 2.2 | Complete I-9, W-4, direct deposit, benefits enrollment | HR |
| 2.3 | Review handbook, policies, confidentiality, security | HR |
| 2.4 | IT setup: email, Clio, phone, VPN, MFA | IT |
| 2.5 | Assign first tasks; introduce to supervisor | Supervisor |
| 2.6 | Lunch with team or buddy | Office Manager |

### Week 1

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 3.1 | Role-specific training (Clio, intake process, billing, etc.) |
| 3.2 | Shadow experienced colleague in same role |
| 3.3 | Review escalation paths and who to ask for what |
| 3.4 | Check-in at end of week: questions, concerns |

### Weeks 2–4

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 4.1 | Gradually increase independent work |
| 4.2 | 30-day check-in: feedback, adjustment |
| 4.3 | Confirm all systems access working |
| 4.4 | Document any process improvements from new hire questions |

---

## Checklist (Printable)

**Pre-Day 1**
- [ ] Welcome email sent
- [ ] IT accounts requested (email, Clio, phone)
- [ ] Workspace ready
- [ ] Onboarding packet prepared
- [ ] Buddy assigned

**Day 1**
- [ ] I-9, W-4, benefits completed
- [ ] Handbook and policies reviewed
- [ ] IT setup complete
- [ ] Supervisor introduction
- [ ] First tasks assigned

**Week 1**
- [ ] Role training completed
- [ ] Shadowing completed
- [ ] Week 1 check-in

**30 Days**
- [ ] 30-day check-in
- [ ] All systems confirmed
- [ ] Feedback documented
