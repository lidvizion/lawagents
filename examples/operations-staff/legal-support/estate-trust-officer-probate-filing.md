# Estate / Trust Officer: Probate Filing Preparation

**Role:** [Estate / Trust Officer (Non-Attorney)](../../../roles/estate-trust-officer/README.md)  
**Frequency:** As needed (when probate petition filed)  
**Approval:** Attorney reviews before filing; court approves

---

## Scenario

Decedent's estate requires probate. Estate/Trust Officer gathers documents, prepares probate petition and inventory, and coordinates with attorney for filing.

---

## Prerequisites

- Death certificate obtained
- Will (if any) located
- Attorney has confirmed probate is required
- Heirs and beneficiaries identified

---

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Document Gathering

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1.1 | Obtain death certificate (certified copies) |
| 1.2 | Locate and copy will (original to court) |
| 1.3 | Gather asset information (bank statements, deeds, titles) |
| 1.4 | Identify heirs, beneficiaries, and their contact info |
| 1.5 | Collect creditor information if known |

### 2. Petition Preparation

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 2.1 | Draft probate petition per state/local forms |
| 2.2 | Complete inventory of assets (real property, accounts, personal property) |
| 2.3 | Prepare notice forms for heirs and creditors |
| 2.4 | Use firm templates; attorney reviews before filing |

### 3. Attorney Review

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 3.1 | Submit draft petition and inventory to attorney |
| 3.2 | Incorporate revisions |
| 3.3 | Obtain attorney approval and signature |

### 4. Filing and Notice

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 4.1 | File petition with probate court |
| 4.2 | Pay filing fees (from estate or client) |
| 4.3 | Serve notice on heirs and creditors per rules |
| 4.4 | Obtain letters of administration or similar |
| 4.5 | Document in matter file |

### 5. Post-Filing Administration

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 5.1 | Open estate bank account if needed |
| 5.2 | Track assets and liabilities |
| 5.3 | Prepare accountings for court as required |
| 5.4 | Coordinate with attorney on distributions |
