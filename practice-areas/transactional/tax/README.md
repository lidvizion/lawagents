# Tax (Advisory & Controversy)

**Focus:** Tax compliance, planning, IRS disputes. Advisory (planning, structuring) and controversy (audits, appeals, litigation).

---

## What Tax Attorneys Do

- **Tax planning:** Entity structure, transactions, estate tax, international
- **Compliance:** Tax filings, opinions, private letter rulings
- **IRS disputes:** Audits, appeals, Tax Court, refund litigation
- **Tax controversy:** Penalties, interest, collection defense
- **Transactional support:** Tax aspects of M&A, real estate, restructuring
- **State and local:** SALT; multi-state issues

---

## How to Operate

### 1. Research and Authority

- **Tax code databases:** IRC, regulations, revenue rulings
- **Legal research:** Lexis, Westlaw, Tax Notes, CCH
- **IRS resources:** IRS.gov, forms, instructions, publications
- **State resources:** State tax agencies, forms

### 2. Accounting Integration

- **QuickBooks/Xero:** Client financial data for tax analysis
- **Accounting software APIs:** Pull data for returns, planning
- **CPA coordination:** Many clients have CPAs; coordinate on filings
- **Workpapers:** Document assumptions, calculations

### 3. IRS and Court Deadlines

- **Statute of limitations:** Refund claims; assessment
- **Filing deadlines:** Extensions; estimated tax
- **Audit response:** IDR deadlines; 30-day letters
- **Tax Court:** Petition deadlines; procedural rules

### 4. Document and Calculation Management

- **Sensitive data:** Tax returns, financials—strict confidentiality
- **Calculations:** Document methodology; audit trail
- **Engagement letters:** Scope; fees; confidentiality
